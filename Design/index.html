PERFECT FIT GURRANTEE

Measure with
Confidence

Getting your measurements right is the first step to a perfectly tailored garment. Follow our simple guide to ensure a flawless fit.

1

Use a Soft Measuring Tape

Ensure the tape is snug but not too tight, and keep it parallel to the floor for accurate measurements.

2

Wear Minimal Clothing

For the most accurate results, measure over lightweight, form-fitting clothing or undergarments.

3

Stand Naturally

Keep your body relaxed with feet together and arms at your sides for consistent measurements.

4

Double Check Key Areas

Focus on chest, waist, hips, inseam, and sleeve length - these define your garment's silhouette.
